Lunar Society

The Lunar Society is our take on a DAO: a builders club and grant program for community members coordinated (and funded) by the PROOF team, but controlled by you. Its goals are twofold—to further the Moonbirds and Mythics brands, and to enhance the holder experience. All Moonbirds holders are automatically voting (and proposing, should you choose to be) members of the Lunar Society.
