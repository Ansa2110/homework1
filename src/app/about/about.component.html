WHO WE ARE

Moonbirds: an art collector’s PFP

We’re in the midst of a cultural revolution—a digital renaissance—in which art is being transformed into something everyone can appreciate, own, and share. Our goal is to create unique experiences for collectors to connect with artists to own and champion their art. And to have a lot of fun along the way.

MOONBIRDS NFT

Art, but also tech

Moonbirds NFTs are a 10,000 piece collection of digital avatars, created by artist and
PROOF co-founder Justin Mezzell. They are distributed under a creative commons (CC0)
licence, meaning that any and every creative can use the artwork to build their own
collections and products.

Fully "in-chain"

The pixelated art is fully on-chain (aka “in-chain”)—meaning that the images are
rendered directly from the Ethereum smart contracts. This unlocks a world of
possibilities for customization.

Custom backgrounds

Not only do we have commemorative backgrounds by Moonbirds artist, Justin
Mezzell, but we collab with artists like XCOPY and Des Lucréce for collectors of their
work.
